Field Prophet

Field Prophet is an iOS app that assists farmers in managing crop disease risks by predicting potential infections in agronomic fields and turf settings. It utilizes statistical models combined with local weather data to forecast diseases like tar spot in corn and white mold in soybeans, enabling timely scouting and treatment decisions.
